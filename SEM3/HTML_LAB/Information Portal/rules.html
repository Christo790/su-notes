Rules

* Classes start from 9:30 a.m. and may continue up to 5:00 p.m. on all the five days of a week (Tuesday to Saturday). Students are advised to come to the Institute by 9:25 a.m. to attend classes.

* As per university rules, 75% attendance is mandatory to appear in semester-end examinations.

* Students must adhere to the Dress Code of the Institute.

* Use of mobile phones in classrooms, library, labs, workshop areas, etc., is strictly prohibited.

* Every student must carry his/her Identity Card while entering the campus and identify himself/herself whenever asked.
